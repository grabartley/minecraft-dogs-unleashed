A Minecraft Fabric mod that adds new dog breeds and dog-related items to enhance the player's experience with their
canine companions.

## Features

- Multiple new dog breeds with unique appearances and behaviors.
- New dog-related items such as dog beds, toys, and food bowls.
- Enhanced interactions between players and their dogs.
